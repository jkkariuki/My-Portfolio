About Me

I am a junior developer and an education professional. I decided to learn to code partly because I want to inspire more diverse students to pursue studies and careers in technology. I am passionate about closing the educational opportunity and career achievement gaps that exist in our country. The technology industry, which has famously struggled to find and keep diverse talent, offers one way to help shrink that gap as career prospects improve at an immense rate. As a full stack developer I know that I can speak to students credibly about the opportunities that are piling up in the tech industry, but more importantly I can show students how they can use technology to bring their ideas to life.
